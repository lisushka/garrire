`~badge`: Confers or removes badges (roles) on request.
`~choose`: Choose between several items (separated by `or`).
`~decide`: Ask a question, get an answer.
`~motivate`: Motivational messages as a service.
`~palindrome`: Find the nearest greater palindrome number.
`~pick`: Select a number from a range, or find random items.
`~plot`: Get a random plot/prompt.
`~roll`: Roll some dice by tabletop notation (e.g. `3d20`).

Also see `~help [command]` or `~[command] help` for more details on each.
